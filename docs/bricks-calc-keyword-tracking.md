# Bricks Calc — Strategic Keyword Tracking

This document outlines the high-intent, strategic keywords for Bricks Calc in the US and Peru markets. These terms focus on **feature-intent** (breakdown, affordability, prepayment, refinance, comparison) rather than just generic volume.

## Tracking Guidelines
- **Max length:** 2 words.
- **Goal:** Monitor ranking for users who need more than a basic monthly payment calculator.
- **Priority:** Feature-intent keywords that differentiate Bricks Calc (calm, utility-focused, no ads).
